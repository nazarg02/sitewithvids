What is SMT Fill?

SMT Fill is a crack in correlation within price, and it is shown within closely correlated assets. The crack in correlation occurs when there is a gap (FVG) within price and one asset fills the gap, whereas another closely correlated asset fails to return back to the gap.

(Use it as an additional confirmation for your setup)

e.g. DC SSMT + 15M PSP + 5M SMT Fill

The gap must be created at exactly the same time within both closely correlated assets.

— SMT Fill
