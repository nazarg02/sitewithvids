Last updated: April 28, 2026

1. Who We Are

Kenpachi Trades ("we", "us", "our") operates the website kenpachitrades.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our site.

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Email address — when you enter our weekly giveaway

4. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymized usage statistics. Google Analytics may set cookies on your device. You can opt out by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Our site contains links to third-party services including:

6. Cookies

We use sessionStorage (not cookies) to remember if you have seen the giveaway popup in your current browser session. This data is never transmitted to our servers and is deleted when you close the browser tab.

Google Analytics uses cookies to track sessions. See section 4 for how to opt out.

7. Data Retention

Giveaway emails are retained only for the duration of the giveaway campaign and deleted thereafter. Analytics data is retained per Google Analytics default settings (up to 14 months).

8. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your email address at any time by contacting us via Discord. We will process your request within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Our site is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
