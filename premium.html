Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get access after subscribing? +
After successful payment, you will be automatically added to the private section of the Discord (make sure you have claimed the role in the Whop Premium section). If you don't receive access, please contact support through Whop.
What exactly is included in Premium? +
Everything on this page: live trading sessions, the private Espada Discord, Advanced QT Modules, the QT Zero-to-Mastery course, the private models, prop firm strategies and the QT indicator. You also keep everything from the free tier.
Do I get the QT indicator with Premium? +
Yes. On the Monthly plan the indicator stays active while your subscription is active. On the Lifetime plan you get lifetime access to the indicator with no recurring payments.
What's the difference between Monthly and Lifetime? +
The content is the same. Monthly is $49/month and everything stays active while you're subscribed. Lifetime is a single $499 payment for permanent access to Premium and lifetime access to the QT indicator, with no renewals.
Can I cancel at any time? +
Yes. Cancel anytime from your account dashboard. You retain access until the end of your current billing period.
Is the free content really free? +
Yes. All free content is completely free. Many studies and materials are available for free access with no additional fees.
