What is Quarterly Theory?

Quarterly Theory (QT) is a time-based market framework that divides price delivery into structured cycles — quarterly, monthly, weekly, and intraday. By understanding which cycle phase price is in, traders can identify high-probability entry windows with clear bias and defined risk. This guide covers everything from the foundational concepts to prop firm-focused execution.
