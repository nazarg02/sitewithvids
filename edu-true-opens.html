What is True Opens?

True Open in Quarterly Theory is the opening price of Q2 in every time cycle. Quarterly Theory divides time into repeating cycles (yearly, monthly, weekly, daily, sessions), each split into 4 quarters (Q1–Q4). The True Open is always the start of Q2 — it serves as a powerful time-based filter to spot manipulation, fake reversals, Judas swings, and stop hunts.

We use them as the key level to define Premium and Discount zones.
